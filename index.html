Polyblocks

A free to mint minimal generative art NFT collection stored on the Polygon blockchain

OpenSea / PolygonScan

Hi, I'm @dandygrape and I made Polyblocks. Why not follow me on Twitter?! I have lots of plans for more cool blockchain stuff!

Polyblocks is my first NFT collection. Every NFT is a combination of blocks uniquely arranged in a 5x5 grid over a colored circle in the background. I really like the clean looks and I really hope that you like it too!

The hearts of Polyblocks are SVGs (scalable vector graphics) that are stored on IPFS. As the name suggests, you can resize these files without loss in quality. That means you can make huge prints of your own Polyblock if you want.

What is Metamask?
Metamask is a wallet for Ethereum-compatible blockchain such as Polygon. There's more info over at metamask.io. You can install it from there, too.
How do I switch to Polygon network?
You can switch networks directly in Metamask. You may have to add Polygon first. There's more info over at polygon.technology. Or you can use chainlist.org to just add ist.
